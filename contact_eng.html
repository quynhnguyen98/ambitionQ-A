Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I inquire regarding orders ?

Please contact us using the inquiry form below or order_gd@ambition.ne.jp.
Please include your company name, name, e-mail address, and inquiry details in the e-mail.
We will get back to you within 1-2 business days.

What kind of comminucation tools do you have ?

You can contact us by email, Chatwork, Skype, Zoom, etc. We will consider other tools depending as your wish.

I only have rough images but can I order ?

Sure, you can feel free to ask even if you haven't decided in details.
First of all, we hear you rough images, and then we give you suggestions.
Don't worry, please leave your orders to us.
Ambition has a lot of experience in producing and operating our own contents for 15 years.

What kind of genre of illustration are you good at ?

Our advantages are illustrations of Bishoujo, Ikemen and maidens, mainly Japanese anime and manga taste Among them,
Ambition's original IP character 「萌えCAN ちぇんじ!」Which is a Bishoujo illustration, has been distributed for 10 years,
and Ikemen illustration 「擬人カレシ」 has been distributed for 9 years.
We are able to make use of our experience of the track record of our own titles, we can draw characters that would be loved for years.
Please check the portfolio on the site for details.

Do you create a medium used other than games ?

Yes, it is possible. We have a lot of achivements of supporting Vtuber design and Yuru-Chara modeling,
so we are happy to work for media other than games.

Please show me your portfolios of IP (intellectual property) illustration ?

Specific work names and collaborators will be available for presentation after the NDA is signed, so we are not able to tell you right here.
However we have many acheivments of producing and collaborating with a famous IP, and has been well received by copyrights who are strict in supervision.
In collaboration, we try to maximize the appeal of IP according to the world view desired by copyrights holders.

What kind of creating tool and delivery format do you have ?

Illustration introduces Photoshop, SAI, CLIP STUDIO PAINT PRO, Illustrator, and the delivery format can be jpg, png, ai, psd (psb), bmp, etc.
Motion introduces Live2D, Spine, SpriteStudio, Live2D can be output as a file format according to modeling or animation,
Spine can be output as JSON, Binary, SpriteStudio as gif or avi.

Is it okay to modify the data by myself after delivery ?

Yes, you are free to edit and retouch it after delivery.
(The copyright will also be transferred to clients) We can also give you an editable file format, so please feel free to ask us.

When is the payment date ?

As a general rule, we decide closing at the end of the month, payment at the end of the following month.
